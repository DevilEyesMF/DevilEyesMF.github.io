Ahoy there!

It appears the waves have led you to my About Me page, so I suppose I owe you a tale or two about who I am and how this journey began.

So... hi! Nice to meet you :)

My name is Remko "Pirate" Claes, a Belgian adventurer behind the lens, living near Mechelen. I'm currently studying Electronics-ICT at Thomas More, and this September, I'll be setting course to Antwerp, where I will start a photography course at Syntra, which I'm incredibly excited about!
Photography has been a hobby so far, but it's a dream of mine to turn this passion into something more
I've mainly shot wildlife and motorsports up until now, but I aim to explore many of the different genres photography has to offer.
Next stop: Event/Concert photography.

Thanks for dropping by! Feel free to continue reading, explore my gallery, or reach out.

Some people like Cowboys...

Let's address the elephant in the room:
Why the hat? Why the pirate theme?

The answer is simple: I just like it. That's it.
Some children are into knights, others into cowboys or firefighters. The list goes on. For me? It's always been pirates.

So when the opportunity came to get a custom-made pirate hat, I didn't have to think twice.
Was it the most financially sound decision I've ever made? Absolutely not.
Do I regret it? Not even for a second.
